Hospital Facility


Our hospital provides 24/7 ambulance service, CT scan, ventilator, and operation room facilities for emergency and critical care. We also offer general and private wards, eye care, teeth care, and canteen services for patients and their families. Additionally, we have ATM and X-ray machines on site, as well as a parking area for convenience. Our hospital is committed to providing comprehensive and patient-centric care. We strive to create a safe and comfortable environment for our patients. Our team of medical professionals is dedicated to delivering high-quality care. We are here to serve you and your loved ones.
